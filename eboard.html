Recruitment!

Join China Care! Apply to be an E-board member or join one of the China Care Teams! We are looking for people to join our Fundraising, Marketing, and Dumplings Teams!

New e-board members begin the semester as China Care Representatives. Throughout the fall semester, as they adjust to the eboard and see the way we work, they choose positions or teams to be apprenticed to. Then as we move into the spring semester, everyone has more developed roles in the club.

We encourage applicants from all class years to apply! Positions are very flexible and we're always looking for diversity to our group!

If you have any questions about positions, email us at tuftschinacare@gmail.com.
