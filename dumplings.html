Dumplings

Dumplings is a playgroup for children who have been adopted from China by American families. Tufts China Care holds themed monthly events to help the children connect to their heritage through crafts, lessons and games. The playgorup also allows the parents and chilren to form a network with other families that attend the group. The college students take on mentor/big sibling roles for each child to serve as their role models and help the children learn more about and appreciate their culture.

The tentative playgroup dates for the 2014-2015 school year will be posted soon!

If you are interested in participating in Dumplings as either a Tufts student a family, don't hesitate to email us at tuftsdumplings@gmail.com. You will be added to our mailing list and notified about upcoming events!
